Beef Stew Recipe

Description

This recipe for beef stew is a comfort meal and it is perfect for cold weather days.It is perfectly served with warm dinner rolls or on a bed of hot rice.

Ingredients

  • Two (2) pounds of beef cut and cubed
  • Three (3) tablespoons all purpose flour
  • Half (1/2) teaspoon garlic powder
  • One (1) onion chopped
  • Half (1/2) teaspoon salt to taste
  • Half (1/2) teaspoon blackpepper to taste
  • Six (6) cups of beef broth
  • Three (3) tablespoons olive oil
  • One (1) pound potatoes peeled and cubed
  • Half (1/2) cup of red wine of choice
  • Four (4) carrots cut into one (1) inch size
  • Two (2) tablespoons cornstarch
  • Two (2) tablespoons water
  • One (1) teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Three (3) tablespoons tomato paste
  • Three quarter (3/4) cup peas

Steps

  1. Combine flour, garlic powder and salt & pepper. Toss beef in flour mixture.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven or pot. Cook the beef and onions until browned. Add beef broth and red wine while scraping up any brown bits in the pan.
  3. Stir in all remaining ingredients except for peas, cornstarch and water. Reduce heat to medium low, cover and simmer 1 hour or until beef is tender (up to 90 minutes).
  4. Mix equal parts cornstarch and water to create a slurry. Slowly add the slurry to the boiling stew to reach desired consistency
  5. Stir in peas and simmer 5-10 minutes before serving . Season with salt & pepper to taste.
